Ingredients:
Tea powder 1.5 tsp
Sugar 1.5 tsp
water 0.25 cups
Chai masala 1 small tsp
Milk 0.75 tsp

In a pan, on a high flame, bring water to boil. Add tea, reduce the flame to low, cover the pan and let it seep for a couple of minutes. Now add sugar and chai masala and let it seep for a 1 more minutes. Finally, add milk and bring to boil again.

Strain chai into a cup and enjoy.
