Ingredients:
Tea powder 1.5 tsp
Sugar 1.5 tsp
water 0.25 cups
Chai masala 1 small tsp
Milk 0.75 tsp

In a pan, on a high flame, bring water to boil. Add tea reduce the flame to low, cover the pan and let it seep. Now add sugar and chai masala and let it seep for a few more minutes. Finally, add milk and bring to boil again.

Strain the chain into a cup and enjoy.
